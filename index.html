The Mahesh Babu Story

From child artist to Telugu cinema's beloved superstar

Mahesh Babu

Ghattamaneni Mahesh Babu

August 9, 1975 Chennai, India

One of Indian cinema's most bankable stars, Mahesh Babu has redefined Telugu cinema with his unique blend of mass appeal and class acting.

Early Career

Debuted as a child artist at age 4 in Needa (1979) and appeared in 8 more films before making his lead debut in Rajakumarudu (1999), winning the Nandi Award for Best Male Debut.

Breakthrough

The 2006 blockbuster Pokiri became the highest-grossing Telugu film at the time, establishing him as a top star. He followed this with hits like Dookudu, Srimanthudu, and Bharat Ane Nenu.

Personal Life

Married to former actress Namrata Shirodkar since 2005. They have two children - Gautam and Sitara. Known for maintaining an exceptionally private personal life despite his stardom.

Philanthropy

Through his Heal Foundation, he's contributed significantly to healthcare and education. Adopted Burripalem village after his film Srimanthudu to implement rural development projects.

"Success is not about being the best, it's about being better than yesterday."
